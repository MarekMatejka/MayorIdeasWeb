Support details

General ideas and improvements

If you have any ideas for improvement, new features, typos or something else that should be changed please send me an email to support@ereception.eu


Examples include:

  • I would like a different chart view.
  • I just spotted a typo on one of the screens.
Bugs and non-critical problems

If you see any functionality that does not work and it is not a critical functionality please send me an email to support@ereception.eu


Examples include:

  • Charts are not correctly showing today's data.
  • When I click on a recipient it shows an error/nothing happens.
Critical errors

If the system or a critical functionality is not working text/call me on +44 7784 361 380.


Examples include:

  • The whole system crashed and I cannot do anything.
  • I cannot collect/hand out parcels.
